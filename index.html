This cozy Uptown cafeteria is a sure win no matter if you’re swinging by for breakfast, lunch, Happy Hour or dinner. With one of the most knowledgeable wait staffs in the industry, you will be guided through our delicious menu of hearty meals & divine desserts while you soak up the ambiance.
